Pizza Dough Recipe

NY Style Pizza at Home

A wonderful recipe for NY Style Pizza Dough

Ingredients

The Poolish Day 1
  • 50g Warm water
  • 1g Active dry yeast
  • 50g Bread or AP flour
  • A pinch of Sugar
Dough Day 2
  • 180g Warm water
  • 4g Active dry yeast
  • A pinch of Sugar
  • 10g Sugar
  • 315g Bread flour
  • 8g Salt
  • 10g Olive Oil

Step by Step instructions

The Poolish Day 1
  1. Mix up into a paste like texture
  2. Place in Container, Cover it up
  3. Leave out in Room Temp for 24 hours
Dough Day 2
  1. Add Warm Water and Active Dry Yeast and a pinch of Sugar to a Container Stir it up, let it sit for about 15 minutes
  2. After 15 minutes, you should see bubbled in the water
  3. Add Oil & the Poolish from yesterday, and stir it
  4. Add the Bread Flour, Salt, Sugar and Olive Oil and mix/stir for 10 to 20 minutes
  5. Knead it the dough to make it smooth
  6. Form a big ball
  7. Cover up the Dough Ball with a Container or Bowl and let it ferment for 45 Minutes
  8. Divide it up to about 300G each
  9. Form 2 Dough Balls
  10. Place it in a sealed container that is oiled, place lid on it, and let it proof in the Fridge for 1 to 5 days
  11. Take the Pizza dough out, and place a damp towel over it while it is in the bowl or container, and let it sit out for about 1 hour or when it reaches room temperature
  12. Form your pizza dough and make a pizza pie!

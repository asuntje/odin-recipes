Recipe for Lasagna Bolognese

A very tasty and easy way to make Lasagna at home

Ingredients for 4 persons

The Bolognesesauce
  • 500g mixed minced meat
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bundle of parsley
  • 1EL tomato paste
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • some olive oil
  • some red wine
The Bechamelsauce
  • 500ml milk
  • 30g butter
  • 40g flour
  • salt & pepper
  • lemon juice
  • nutmeg
The Lasagna
  • Butter for the form
  • 300g Lasagna pasta plates
  • CHEESE
  • Butter flakes

Step by Step instructions

For the Bolognesesauce
  1. Heat the olive oil in a high frying pan
  2. Fry the minced meat in the pan
  3. Add sliced onions and parsley to the pan
  4. Add fine sliced garlic and tomato paste to the mix
  5. Pour the canned tomatoes and add pepper and salt
  6. If desired add some red wine at last
  7. leave it opened for 30 minutes on medium heat
For the Bechamelsauce
  1. Heat the butter in a small pan
  2. Mix the flour into the butter until bright yellow without burning it
  3. Add milk and keep stirring
  4. Keep the sauce cooking on low heat for 30 minutes
  5. Finalize the sauce with salt, pepper, lemon juice and nutmeg
The Lasagna
  1. Add butter to a form and put a layer of bolognese sauce on the bottom
  2. Add lasagna pasta plates ontop
  3. Add a layer of bolognese sauce again
  4. Add a layer of bechamel sauce
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 until the form is fully filled
  6. The last layer should be bechamel sauce
  7. Last we add a lot of cheese and some butter flakes
  8. The Lasagna should go 30-40 minutes in the oven at 180 degrees celsius

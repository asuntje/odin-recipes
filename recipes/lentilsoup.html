Lentil Soup Recipe

Recipe for traditional German Lentil Soup

Delicious and easy Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of Lentils
  • 8 potatoes
  • 4 Mettenden
  • 200g of bacon cubes
  • 600ml Vegetable broth
  • Salt & Pepper

Step by Step instructions

  1. Put some oil in a pot on medium heat
  2. Add the bacon to the oil and let it fry for 10 minutes without burning it
  3. Add canned lentils to the pot
  4. Add about half the canned lenthils as veggy broth
  5. Add sliced Mett sausages to the soup
  6. Let the soup cook for 45 minutes
  7. After 45 minutes add potato cubes and let it cook for another 15 minutes
  8. At last we can add salt & pepper
